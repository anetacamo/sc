## Young artists debut in abundance – and we exhibit the youth's on take on the most urgent political agendas on artistic election posters

Join us at the artistic, youth celebration that is OUTPUT Festival. As a part of the festival, we will exhibit a bunch of election posters created by young Aarhusians as a political commentary on the usual aesthetics of politics – showcasing their most urgent political agendas. 

The posters have been developed through three workshops at Ungdomskulturhuset - UKH, Aarhus Billede- og Medieskole, and Skolen for Kunst og Design. 

You can experience the election posters at two exhibitions:
- at _OUTPUT Festival on October 22-23_ the posters from UKH and Aarhus Billede- og Medieskole will be shown.
- at _the two political meetings on UKH on November 1 and 3_ all of the posters will be exhibited.
