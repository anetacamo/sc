## YOUNG CITIZENS AND THEIR POLITICIANS IN THE BLENDER.

_A 3-hour session will get young citizens and political candidates to work together at eye level._

We will take advantage of the diversity of the participants to create an exciting framework, and to discover new ideas and concerns. By the end of the session, young citizens will have engaged in concrete political cases with a host of young up-coming politics, and the candidates will have had a more personal interaction with their represented.
